Description

Vines

Kirsten Regalado’s “Vines” is a matching dress that showcases an ornate white cauliflory pattern over the radiant red fabric. It resembles a Mexican traditional dress, also called as Huipil, with an updated twist that only Kirsten Regalado can deliver: The blouse is a plunging sleeveless crop top with micro net fabric for its sleeves that reach halfway the forearms, while the skirt has a similar fabric and design with the blouse, with larger patterns that ended at the thick base that serve as the gown’s hem.
