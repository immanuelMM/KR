Colorful Marine

This gown was worn by Miss Universe Mauritius 2021, Vandana Jeetah, during the Miss Universe 2021. Kirsten Regalado’s “Colorful Marine'' piece reflects the colorful corals immersed in the cool blue ocean, overwhelmed with the bright ray of light revealing an encircling school of fish. In the gown structure, the colorful corals create stunning visuals at the hem that is folded fashionably forming a fish tail. The cool blue ocean clasps the shapely cuts covering the limbs up to the waist, baring the subdued design of encircling school of fish that wraps the bosom.
